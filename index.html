About

Pursuing a Doctor of Music in Piano Performance at Florida State University under the mentorship of Read Gainsford, Qinling brings a rich musical background spanning three countries, three distinguished conservatories, and two languages. Her artistry combines rigorous technical command with deeply expressive musicality, shaped by studies with renowned pedagogues across China and the United States.

With over three years of private and institutional teaching experience, she has guided students of all ages through ABRSM examinations, consistently producing Distinction and Merit results. Her bilingual fluency in English and Mandarin Chinese allows her to connect with diverse students and families, making classical piano accessible across cultural boundaries.
